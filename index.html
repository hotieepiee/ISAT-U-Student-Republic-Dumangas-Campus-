About Evergreen Academy

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character while preparing students for success in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Community - these core values guide everything we do and shape our school culture.

Academic Programs

Elementary School

Foundation years focusing on literacy, numeracy, and social skills through play-based learning and hands-on activities.

Middle School

Transitional years emphasizing critical thinking, project-based learning, and character development with specialized subject teachers.

High School

Advanced academic preparation with AP courses, college counseling, and leadership opportunities for university readiness.

STEM Programs

Cutting-edge science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education with state-of-the-art laboratories and equipment.

Arts & Culture

Comprehensive arts education including visual arts, music, drama, and creative writing to foster artistic expression.

Athletics

Competitive sports programs and physical education promoting teamwork, discipline, and healthy lifestyle choices.
